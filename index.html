Talk preparation

Most attendees will give a talk on their current research. Faculty, Postdocs, and PhD students will give 10 minute talks. Masters and Undergraduate students will give 2 minute lightning talks.

We will be using a single laptop on the day for all talks to avoid losing time between speakers. Please name your presentation with your name, e.g. Jane_Doe.key and provide us with BOTH the presentation file and a version that has been exported to PDF. We have prepared several talk templates:

  • Keynote, [link]
  • Powerpoint, [link]
  • Google Slides, [link]

Lightning talk specific guidelines

Lightning talks should consist of at most three slides: a title slide, a project description slide, and a results slide. If animations cause these three slides to become more than three when exported to PDF that's fine. We will be combining the lightning talks into one long PDF so no time is lost between presentations.
